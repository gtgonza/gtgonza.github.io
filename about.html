Gonzalo González is a BA in Economics and Administration graduate and MA in Applied Economics student attending the Pontifica Universidad Católica de Chile. Gonzalo has worked as a TA and academic mentor in the following courses: intro to economics, intro to microeconomics, precalculus, accounting, economic analysis, monetary macroeconomics and labor economics. Also, he has worked as a research assistant, data analyst and consultant. Gonzalo is interested in labor economics, urban economics and inequality topics.
