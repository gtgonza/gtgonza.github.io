Profile

I'm an analyst at QA Consultores in Santiago, working across the problems that need microeconomics and econometrics done carefully: competition policy, tariff regulation, demand estimation and tariff design, corporate finance, environmental and natural-resource economics, agricultural economics, and the social and private appraisal of projects.

My work has supported firms across energy, water utilities, telecommunications, manufacturing, road-infrastructure concessions and agribusiness — most often where the analysis has to stand up before regulators, technical panels or courts.
